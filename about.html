About Leveraging 25+ years of experience

Who we Are

Specialize in crafting
custom, high-end solutions

We at Acube Industries, part of the Acube Group, specialize in woodwork, joinery, furniture manufacturing, interior designing, and fit-out.

Leveraging 25+ years of experience, our bespoke, custom, sustainable, and enduring interior craftsmanship caters to residential, commercial, corporate, hospitality, entertainment, and several other spaces.

Our cutting-edge facility alongside a team of 250+ skilled workforce, is dedicated to delivering top-tier, durable, and stunning products. Our network of trusted worldwide partners enables us to accommodate varied and complex projects of varied scales, blending flexibility, form, and functionality, without compromising design.
